## Render Discipline

### Above The Fold

Must render:

- H1.
- Hero paragraph.
- Primary CTA: `Send us your brief`.
- Proof-card navigation with all case anchors.
- One featured proof card, preferably HCT Graduation Program or Sadia Carrefour Rollout.

Do not render above the fold:

- Full case-study text for every case.
- Source caveats.
- Money Kicks as equal-weight hero proof.
- FAQ.

### Render As Visual, Not Text

- Case navigation should be cards with service tags and one metric.
- Featured case should use large image/stat treatment.
- Anchored case sections should be broken into short labeled blocks.
- Situation/Pressure/What EGS Did/Result/What It Proves can be visible labels, but each answer should stay short.
- Public source notes should be captions/footnotes.
- Money Kicks should be a smaller credibility card.

### Show, Do Not Tell

- HCT: show ceremony count, graduates, guests, locations, and relationship length.
- Fujairah: show `10 hours`, `5-6 metre extension`, `Zayed Sports Complex`.
- Sadia: show `33 locations`, `13 vehicles`, `midnight-before 6am`, `QA/QC`.
- Philips: show `200 sqm`, `10-12 hours`, `ultrasound display`.
- Kazakhstan: show `168 sqm`, `5-6 chillers`, `Gulfood 2026`.
- Money Kicks: show only name-recognition credibility unless more detail is confirmed.

### Guidance Only, Do Not Render

- `Page Layout Rules`
- `Case Study Format`
- `Image direction`
- `Guardrail`
- `Public Source Handling`
- `What Not To Say`
- `Implementation Notes`

## Page Layout Rules

- Lead with the proof-card grid/navigation.
- Use one featured case at the top: HCT Graduation Program or Sadia Carrefour Rollout.
- Use anchored case sections below the cards.
- Keep each case visually broken into short labeled blocks.
- Use real images or factual image placeholders for each case.
- Money Kicks / Money Kickz should be visibly lighter than the fully detailed cases until more information is confirmed.
- Add service filters or simple jump links if the layout can support it without redesigning the site.

## Case Study Format

Each case uses:

- Situation
- Pressure
- What EGS Did
- Result
- What It Proves
- Relevant Service
- CTA

Keep each full case between 180-260 words.

Each case also needs:

- Service tag,
- image direction,
- one proof line that can be reused as a card,
- one buyer takeaway.

Proof line:

```text
A 5-6 metre stage extension requested 10 hours before ceremony at Zayed Sports Complex.
```

### Sadia Carrefour Rollout {#sadia-carrefour-rollout}

Service tag: `Retail rollout`

Proof line:

```text
33 Carrefour hypermarket locations completed between midnight and before 6am.
```

Image direction: chiller branding, island display, hypermarket/mall installation, or retail campaign imagery if available.

Situation:

```text
In 2019, Sadia had planned its Carrefour hypermarket retail installations for Friday.
```

Pressure:

```text
On Wednesday, the client asked EGS to move the rollout forward and complete all 33 Carrefour hypermarket locations across the UAE that same night. Mall work could only begin after closing.
```

What EGS Did:

```text
EGS started around midnight and finished before 6am. Scope included chiller branding and installation, plus island displays. The rollout used 13 vehicles, one labourer per vehicle, and 8-10 QA/QC people moving across teams, with approximately 25-30 people involved overall.
```

Result:

```text
All 33 locations were completed before morning.
```

What It Proves:

- multi-location rollout capacity,
- overnight retail execution,
- QA/QC coordination,
- hypermarket/mall access discipline.

Buyer takeaway:

```text
For retail teams, the reassurance is not only speed. It is vehicles, access timing, team split, and QA/QC across locations before customers arrive.
```

## Public Source Handling

Use source notes carefully:

- HCT official articles can support ceremony context, venues, and student counts where cited in the Content Bible.
- Nstands/Neventum can support third-party directory proof for EGS existence/profile, not editorial endorsement.
- Do not describe EGS's HCT production role as press-covered unless a source names EGS directly.
- On the website, keep source notes short. The case story should still be readable for a buyer.
