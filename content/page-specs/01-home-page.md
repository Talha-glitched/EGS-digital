# 01 Home Page Content Spec

## Page Goal

Make EGS feel like the fast, capable Dubai production house for high-stakes physical brand moments, then route visitors quickly into the right service or proof page.

## Audience

Primary: marketing managers, exhibition/event leads, procurement contacts.  
Secondary: retail/trade marketing teams, institutional ceremony leads, fitout buyers.

## SEO

Title: `Exhibit Graphic Sign | Exhibition Stands, Events, Retail Branding Dubai`  
Meta: `EGS is a Dubai production house for exhibition stands, graduation ceremonies, retail branding, signage, and branded interiors built for deadlines that cannot slip.`

## H1 And Hero

H1:

```text
Exhibition stands, ceremonies, retail rollouts, and branded spaces built for fixed deadlines.
```

Hero paragraph:

```text
EGS is a Dubai production house for high-stakes physical brand moments across the UAE: exhibition stands, graduation ceremonies, retail branding, signage, and branded interiors. When the requirement changes late and the date cannot move, we keep the work moving until it is ready and correct.
```

Primary CTA: `Send us your brief` -> `/contact`  
Secondary CTA: `See case studies` -> `/case-studies`

Visual direction: use a strong collage/grid of real EGS work. HCT, Philips, Sadia/retail, and fitout/retail imagery should appear above the fold or immediately after. Add short factual captions where images can support proof.

## Render Discipline

### Above The Fold

Must render:

- H1.
- Hero paragraph.
- Primary CTA: `Send us your brief`.
- Secondary CTA: `See case studies`.
- Real work collage/grid or one strong project image.
- One immediate proof signal, either client marquee preview or a proof number such as HCT/Sadia.

Do not render above the fold:

- Full process section.
- Full FAQ.
- Long brand-positioning explanation.
- Internal terms like `pressure-tested positioning`.

### Render As Visual, Not Text

- Client names should be a muted marquee, not a paragraph.
- Service routing should be four cards.
- Process should be a compact horizontal stepper.
- Proof should be a scrolling card row or grid with clickable anchors.
- HCT anchor should use large serif stats and one short paragraph.

### Show, Do Not Tell

- Show EGS speed through Sadia: `33 Carrefour locations. Midnight to before 6am.`
- Show scale through HCT: `7 ceremonies. 4,500 graduates. 13,500 guests.`
- Show exhibition adaptation through Philips: `200 sqm stand adapted in 10-12 hours.`
- Show pavilion pressure through Kazakhstan: `168 sqm pavilion, 5-6 added chillers before opening.`

### Guidance Only, Do Not Render

- `Purpose: say what EGS does and why it matters in five seconds.`
- `Must show:`
- `Visual direction:`
- `Do not add project claims in the marquee.`
- `What Not To Say`
- `Implementation Notes`

### 4. Process Snapshot

Heading:

```text
From brief to handover, the work stays physical.
```

Copy:

```text
EGS works backwards from the opening date, showtime, launch window, or handover. The team checks the deadline, location, access, material needs, drawings, and installation constraints before production moves.
```

Steps:

- `Brief`: send the service, date, venue or location, drawings/photos, and what needs to happen.
- `Feasibility`: EGS checks timing, materials, access windows, and risk.
- `Produce`: fabrication, print, branding, sourcing, and team planning move together.
- `Install`: crews work around venue, mall, or event access.
- `Check and hand over`: the physical work is reviewed before opening, launch, or showtime.

Visual direction: keep this tight. Use a horizontal stepper, not a long text block.
